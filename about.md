# About Me

I've always had a passion for art. My initial inspiration to start creating art was my brother. I also learnt more about art because of my parents, since they are also very artistic people. I love exploring different media and styles of art.

I love listening to music. Some of my favorite bands include *Linkin Park*, *Green Day*, *The offspring*, *The Beatles*, *Nirvana*, and *Metallica*. I have always loved how people make music, how they utilize the intruments to their need, and how you can make any music with instruments. Many artists have always fascinated me - how do they make such good music?! To understand this better, I started learning the guitar. I realize that music comes from the heart, and with the skills you can make anything from it.

Since not long ago, I have started taking an interest in *design*. When I was in fifth grade, my father introduced me to a famous designer named Dieter Rams. I took a look at some of his designs , and I realized what an impact his designs really had on the world. An example is his simple design of a calculator which had different coloured buttons to help differentiate them based on function. This calculator design was later implimented in the calculator of Apple products. It was after this when I realized that design is a very important aspect of our everyday life. Everything that we use was designed by someone who thought about how their design will affect the function of the object.

This website is about my journey in art, design, and building useful things.

Maya Mahesh
